Halo Handbook

A guide to Achievements, Skulls, lore, and Easter Eggs in Halo.

Disclaimer! This is a fan-made guide let me know if you find mistakes or want more added. Enjoy!

Collection-Wide Achievements

  • MCC Complete – Unlock all achievements in Halo: The Master Chief Collection.
  • Just Getting Started – Complete 50 missions or multiplayer games.
  • Veteran – Complete 400 missions or multiplayer games.
  • Balaho's Most Wanted – Kill 1000 Grunts.
  • Skulltaker – Collect each campaign skull in each game.
  • LASO Master – Complete all campaigns on Legendary All Skulls On.
  • Let’s Get Started – Play 1 mission or multiplayer game.
  • Feet First – Complete a mission in every Halo game.
  • Stick With It... a Little Longer – Play 50 missions or multiplayer games.
  • Kahuna – Complete 100 missions or multiplayer games.
  • Temper Temper – Kill 100 enemies with melee.
  • Flipyap – Kill 50 Jackals.
  • Beholden – Watch a full credits sequence.
  • Wyrmslayer - play firefight on reach

Halo: Combat Evolved Anniversary

  • Pillar of Autumn - Complete "The Pillar of Autumn" on any difficulty.
  • Halo - Complete "Halo" on any difficulty.
  • Truth and Reconciliation - Complete "Truth and Reconciliation" on any difficulty.
  • The Silent Cartographer - Complete "The Silent Cartographer" on any difficulty.
  • Assault on the Control Room - Complete "Assault on the Control Room" on any difficulty.
  • Pillar of awesome - Beat the par score in Pillar of Autumn.
  • Living Legend – Complete all Halo: CE levels on Legendary difficulty.
  • That Just Happened – Finish "The Library" on Heroic or Legendary difficulty without dying.
  • He’s Unstoppable! – Get a kill streak of 10 or more without dying in multiplayer.
  • Speed Reader – Beat The Library in under 15 minutes.
  • Look Out for the Little Guys – Complete any mission cooperatively for the campaign.
  • Dear Diary... – Find and view a terminal in any mission.

Halo 2: Anniversary

  • The Heretic – Complete "The Heretic" on any difficulty.
  • The Armory – Complete "The Armory" on any difficulty.
  • Cairo Station – Complete "Cairo Station" on any difficulty.
  • Outskirts – Complete "Outskirts" on any difficulty.
  • Trophy Collector – Collect all Halo 2: Anniversary Skulls and Terminals.
  • Legend – Complete all Halo 2: Anniversary Campaign missions on Legendary.
  • Monopolized – Complete the Halo 2 Campaign on Legendary in under 3 hours.
  • Sightseeing – View all Terminals in Halo 2: Anniversary.
  • Back for More – Complete a Halo 2 mission in co-op mode.
  • Bite the Hand – Kill a developer in matchmaking or firefight (community event).

Halo 3

  • Sierra 117 – Complete "Sierra 117" on any difficulty.
  • Crow’s Nest – Complete "Crow's Nest" on any difficulty.
  • The Ark – Complete "The Ark" on any difficulty.
  • Seven on Seven – Enter into a ranked or social match on the 7th of the month.
  • Primate – Find and view all Halo 3 Terminals.
  • Meta – Achieve the par score on any mission in Halo 3 campaign.
  • To the Spoils Go the Victor – Finish the "Covenant" mission without dying.
  • High Altitude – Complete "Halo" without entering any vehicles.
  • Passing the Torch – Complete the campaign co-operatively.
  • Zanzibar! – Play a game on "Zanzibar" map in matchmaking.

Halo 3: ODST

  • Prepare to Drop – Complete "Prepare to Drop" on any difficulty.
  • Tayari Plaza – Complete "Tayari Plaza" on any difficulty.
  • Crowd Control – Kill 7 enemies within 4 seconds of each other in Firefight.
  • Vidmaster Challenge: Endure – In Firefight, pass the 4th set on Heroic or Legendary.
  • Rain or Shine – Beat any Firefight mission solo without dying.
  • Listener – Find all audio logs in ODST campaign.
  • Tourist – Visit all districts in ODST's "Hub" world.

Halo 4

  • Dawn – Complete "Dawn" on any difficulty.
  • Requiem – Complete "Requiem" on any difficulty.
  • Skim the Surface – Finish "Infinity" without entering the Pelican.
  • Midnight Launch – Beat "Midnight" in under 25 minutes.
  • Spoiled Rotten – Find and claim all Terminals in Halo 4.
  • The Legend of 117 – Beat the campaign on Legendary solo.
  • Contact the Domain – Find every hidden terminal in Halo 4 campaign.

Halo: Reach

  • Noble Actual – Complete "Noble Actual" on any difficulty.
  • Winter Contingency – Complete "Winter Contingency" on any difficulty.
  • Lone Wolf – Complete "Lone Wolf" on Legendary difficulty.
  • One final firefight - Get 150,000 score before dying (set it on easy mode).
  • A Monument to All Your Sins – Complete the campaign solo on Legendary difficulty.
  • Skunked – Win a game of Invasion Slayer as a Spartan without letting the Elites score.
  • If They Came to Hear Me Beg – Perform an assassination against an Elite to survive a fall.
  • Keep It Clean – Complete "The Pillar of Autumn" using only the pistol.

Multiplayer & Forge Achievements

  • Crew Member – Complete a multiplayer match.
  • Social Butterfly - Play matches in every multiplayer playlist.
  • Medal Completionist – Earn every medal at least once.
  • Forge Master – Create and publish a map in Forge mode.
  • Game Changer – Make a custom game variant and play it.
  • Head-to-Head – Win a 1v1 duel in multiplayer.
  • Survivor – Win a Free-for-All match without dying.

MCC Seasonal, Playlist, and Miscellaneous Achievements

  • Season Point – Earn your first season point.
  • Challenge Accepted – Complete a Weekly Challenge.
  • Playlist Warrior – Finish all playlist missions in a single Halo title.
  • True Completionist – Complete every campaign, playlist, and challenge in MCC.
  • Legacy – Earn a reward from every MCC season.

DLC Achievements

  • Firefight Master – Complete all Firefight missions on Legendary.
  • Beta Season – Complete all Beta Season challenges.
  • Tough as Nails – Complete the LASO (Legendary All Skulls On) achievement for any game.
  • Title Update Winner – Win a match using a new TU playlist variant.
  • Expansion Explorer – Play a game on every DLC map.
